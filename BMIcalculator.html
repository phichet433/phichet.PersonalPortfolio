BMI Calculator

Description

BMI stands for Body Mass Index. It’s a value derived from an individual’s height and weight. With the help of BMI, we can find out whether an individual’s weight is healthy or not. Let’s have a look at the formula for calculating BMI:

BMI = Weight in Kilograms / (Height in Meters * Height in Meters)
